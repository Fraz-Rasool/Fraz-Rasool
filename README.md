## 📌 Featured Projects

### ASPIRE — Autonomous Simulation, Prediction & Isolated Retrieval Engine (FYP · Ongoing)

An AI-powered enterprise simulation platform leveraging multi-agent architectures and retrieval-augmented intelligence for strategic decision support. Built around autonomous agents specializing in Finance, Marketing, Product, and Executive decision-making.

### DEEN AI — Islamic Knowledge RAG Chatbot

RAG-powered chatbot trained on the Quran, Sahih Bukhari, and Sahih Muslim. Utilizes Pinecone for semantic retrieval across 150,000+ Hadith entries and Google Gemini for grounded response generation. Built with Python, LangChain, FastAPI, and NiceGUI.

### AI-Powered Respiratory Disease Detection System

Machine learning pipeline for classifying respiratory diseases from lung sound recordings. Achieved 81% accuracy and 0.82 weighted F1-score while incorporating Explainable AI techniques such as Grad-CAM, Saliency Maps, and Integrated Gradients.

### NeuroScan — Brain Tumor MRI Classification

Computer vision system for MRI brain tumor analysis combining image preprocessing, feature extraction, and CNN-based classification. Achieved approximately 80% accuracy and 81% precision for benign vs. malignant tumor classification.

### Explainable Movie Recommendation Chatbot

Recommendation system combining Knowledge Representation & Reasoning (KRR) with Retrieval-Augmented Generation. Uses knowledge graphs, SPARQL querying, and Gemini-powered conversational recommendations with transparent reasoning pathways.
